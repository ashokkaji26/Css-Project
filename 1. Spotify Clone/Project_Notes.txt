1. Overflow Css Property :- Jab hmare pass container ke height se jyada content hota h, us case me content box se 
                            bahar jane lagta h, use Overflow kahte h, aur us Overflow ko control karne ke liye
                            hmare pass ek scroll bar hota h, jiske help se hm baki contents ko v scroll karke dekh sakte h.

                   --->  1. overflow: visible; --> content agar box se bahar ja raha h fir v dikhega.
                   --->  2. overflow: hidden; --> jo content box se bahar ja raha h wo hide ho jayega, aur scroll karke v nhi dekh sakte.
                   --->  3. overflow: clip; --> jo content box se bahar ja raha h use cut kar dete h.
                   --->  4. overflow: scroll; --> box ke under scroll karke sare contents ko dekh sakte h.
                   --->  5. overflow: auto; --> isme v hm box ke under scroll karke sare contents ko dekh sakte h.

2. Viewport :- Browser ka wo part jo hme dikhayi deta h.

               under content h, uske alawe jitna baki spaces h wo flex:1; wale element ko dedo.
